fi,j=max⁡0≤k≤∣Si∣{fi−1,j−ik+si,k}f_{i,j}=\max_{0\le k\le |S_i|} \{ f_{i-1,j-ik}+s_{i,k} \} fi,j​=0≤k≤∣Si​∣max​{fi−1,j−ik​+si,k​}
